# MVP Scope (v1)

## Hard Boundaries

1. **Federal MPs only**
   - The MVP includes only elected representatives in the federal Parliament of Canada.
   - No provincial, territorial, municipal, or other local representatives are included in v1.

2. **Single election cycle only**
   - The MVP includes data from exactly one election cycle.
   - For v1, this should be the latest completed federal general election cycle.

3. **Promises data is manual admin entry**
   - Promise records in v1 are entered and maintained manually by admins.
   - There is no automated NLP or machine extraction in v1.

4. **Public pages are read-only**
   - Public-facing pages in v1 are view-only.
   - End users cannot directly edit politician profiles, promises, or tracking fields.

5. **Community features are structured issue reports only**
   - Community participation in v1 is limited to submitting structured issue reports.
   - There are no open forum threads or free-form discussion systems in v1.

## Explicitly Not in v1

- Automated scraping pipeline.
- Sentiment AI.
- Multi-level discussion hierarchy.
- Province/local representatives.
